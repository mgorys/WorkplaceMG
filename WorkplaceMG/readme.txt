This is app when you can make a reservation in office. Firstly you need to add a employee by giving a name in proper tab. 
Then In Reservation tab you can create reservation by choosing your name, place where you want to work and period of time.
Program will find if your reservation will overlap with others or if there will be no equipment(every workplace needs some 
equipment to have a set of devices). You can search other reservation in proper tab, or even filter to find out best date.
